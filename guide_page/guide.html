HOME GUIDE & RESOURCES

HOW TO PLAY

In order to start the game, go to "HOME" and then select "PLAY."

After opening the game menu, select the museum collection you would like to guess from, or click random.

From there, click "SELECT PIECE" and then you will see a random piece from the selected collection/s.

Take a look at the piece and then type in an attribution. Attributions may be in any order.
